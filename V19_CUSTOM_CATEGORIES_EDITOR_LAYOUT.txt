MyNewVoice v19 - Custom categories and editor-style settings

Changes:
- Imported custom top-menu categories from complete backups are now preserved and shown.
- Unknown/custom category IDs are no longer filtered out during import or rendering.
- Content editing now uses a more editor-style table layout inspired by the standalone MyNewVoice Backup Editor.
- Settings top menu is simplified: editing, complete import/export, remove images/audio, refresh app code, and About.
- Content Management now includes menu/phrase text editing plus picture and voice controls in the same carer editor area.
- Backup/export schema is unchanged and remains compatible with v17+ complete backups and the separate editor.
