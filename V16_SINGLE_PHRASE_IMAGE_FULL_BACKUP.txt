MyNewVoice v16 - single phrase image and complete backup

Changes:
- Photo / Voice Setup wording now explains where the selected picture will appear.
- Top menu images are cropped square and downsized before local storage.
- Phrase photos use one image only: it appears beside the phrase and in the spoken popup.
- My People photos are cropped circular.
- Separate phrase zoom image setup has been removed from the setup workflow. Legacy zoom images may still display if already present.
- Images are downsized/cropped at selection time to reduce storage and backup size.
- Settings now includes password-protected complete backup export/import.
- Complete backup preserves menu/phrase content, menu order/visibility, local photos, and recorded voices.
- Backup files use the .mnvoice extension and are intended to work on iPhone/iPad, Android, and PC.
