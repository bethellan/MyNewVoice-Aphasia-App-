MyNewVoice v28 - Mobile Touch Interaction Lock

Small permanent touch-interaction fix for hold-to-speak on iOS and Android.

Changes:
- Added CSS to suppress text selection, iOS touch callout, tap highlight, and image dragging on app buttons/cards/phrase rows/images.
- Added installTouchInteractionLock() startup helper.
- The helper blocks contextmenu, selectstart, and dragstart inside the app root, while allowing normal text entry fields.
- Used touch-action: manipulation on app controls; did not add touch-action: none to communication surfaces so scrolling remains available.
- Confirmed existing hold-to-speak logic uses pointerdown, pointerup, pointercancel, and pointerleave.

Unchanged:
- Backup/export/import schema.
- Phrase/category/media IDs and storage keys.
- Dad-facing layout and phrases.
- Speech behaviour, local images/audio, and settings data.
