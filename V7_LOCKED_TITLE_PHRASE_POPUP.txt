MyNewVoice v7 - locked title bar and spoken phrase popup

Changes:
- The top title/speech bar remains fixed in place.
- On the main menu, the title bar says: Select a button to speak...
- Inside a submenu, the title bar displays the selected menu name, such as Food & Drink or Comfort.
- Only the menu cards / phrase rows scroll.
- Tapping a phrase opens a large popup showing the selected sentence.
- The app plays the locally recorded voice if one exists.
- If no recorded voice exists, the device-generated voice is used.
- Optional zoom images now appear inside the spoken phrase popup.
- Existing image naming, local photo setup, local voice recording, backup/import, category colours, and stacked layout are retained.
