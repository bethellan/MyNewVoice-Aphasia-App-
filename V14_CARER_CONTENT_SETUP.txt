MyNewVoice v14 - Carer Content Setup

Changes:
- Rebuilt Content Management as a password-protected carer dashboard.
- Added top menu section editing: rename, hide/show, and reorder.
- Added one-selected-phrase editing: edit text, optional fallback icon, hide/show, reorder, and delete.

Note:
